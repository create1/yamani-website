Explore Our Mission

The Opportunity

Small Cities Are Underserved Innovation Markets

The Migration

Remote workers, founders, and creatives are relocating to smaller, lifestyle-driven towns with strong communities and access to nature.

The Gap

These communities lack structured founder ecosystems, access to capital, professional development, and integrated wellness support.

The Potential

Nevada City represents a high-potential, undercapitalized ecosystem with strong creative culture and growing tech presence.

Yamani exists to bridge this gap through education, community, and opportunity.
